Languages

17 days ago

Language plugins provide translations of the core TiddlyWiki interface.

The following languages are currently available:

  • 中文 (简体)
  • 中文 (正體)
  • Français (France)
  • Deutsch (Österreich)
  • Deutsch (Deutschland)

You can contribute by learning how to translate TiddlyWiki into your language.
